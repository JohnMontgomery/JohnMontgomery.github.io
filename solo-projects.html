Solo Projects

Here are some of the projects that I've worked on my own. These are usually to solve a problem I experience or someone close to me experiences.

Path Finder



This is a program I created based on my problem that I wanted to see scenarios of possible plans of study based on what major and minor I wanted. I didn't want to contact my academic advisor multiple times just for something that I could do myself. However, this proved to be time-consuming so I decided to create this so that any other students that are curious won't have to spend time organizing what classes they've taken and what credits they have already.

This uses a database of each major and minor and the classes required to complete both. Users are allowed to enter credits they have received from other universities or tests such as AP and IB. Users are also given a choice of how many credit hours they want to take a semester. The program then generates a possible table of classes per semester based on the user's preferences.
